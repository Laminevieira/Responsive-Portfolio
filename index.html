About Me

Coach Seck--now entering his fifth season as head coach for the men's soccer team--was a two-year member of the BMCC Men's Soccer team (2008 and 2009) and served as the Co-Captain and Captain respectively. During his coaching tenure at BMCC, he has earned the title of CUNYAC Coach of the Year in 2014 and 2015 before bringing the CUNYAC Championship trophy back to BMCC in his fourth year. Following the completion of his second season on the pitch, Modou Seck was honored as the 2009 CUNY Scholar Athlete of the Year Honorable Mention as well as being named 2nd team All-Region.
